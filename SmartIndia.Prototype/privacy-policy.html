What information do we collect?

The Provider may collect your information so that we can constantly improve our products and provide you with the best possible service. It is important for Users to be aware of the type of information that the Provider collects.

Аggregate Information

The aggregated information that the Provider collects is such data by which it is not possible for a particular user to be to identified. Such sort of information is also: time zone; the connection speed of the user to the servers of the Provider; network connection data, and so on.

Personal Information

We collect personal information when you create an account, update or modify the information in your account, buy service, fill out a survey, subscribe to the Email Updates, send us emails, and/or participate in other services on our Site. We may use the provided personal information in order to respond to your inquiries, to provide specific services that you have selected, or to send you marketing communication after being granted your explicit consent. Part of this information is provided directly by you, for instance, when you create an account at http://smartindia.bestwebonline.net, contact our support, or take part in an online session. Some personal information we receive through your activities on our website and services. We typically collect this information through automated technologies such as cookies. You have the option to control whether or not cookies are accepted by your browser. Most browsers offer instructions on how to disable the use of cookies, although you may not have access to all the features of this website if you do so. See your browser’s “help” button on how to do this. We may also partner with one or more third-party networks to collect information, and protect it according to the practices described in this statement, as well as any additional restrictions imposed by the data source. These sources vary over time, but include the following:

  • Social networks - when you use your existing social network account;
  • Service providers who help us determine the location of your IP address;
  • Partners with whom we offer joint services or are engaged in joint marketing activities;
  • Other.

If you choose not to submit any personal information when requested, we may not be able to fully provide you with certain services and personalized features, and may limit the special offers we can offer. The various types of information that we collect from you depends on the services you use and the way you interact with us. The data we collect may include the following:

  • Personal contact information - we collect your name, email address, telephone, address, and other similar contact details;
  • Identification data - we collect your account login information, password and username, and security email address used to authenticate and access your account;
  • Demographic information - this includes any information that describes your demographic characteristics, such as age, gender, country, and preferred language;
  • Location data - includes your location obtained from your IP address or data that indicates a specified or associated with you physical address;
  • Device data - we collect and use device information through which you access and use our products and services, including information that your browser or the mobile app sends when you are using it. This data may include your unique device identifier, IP address, your browser type and configuration, the date and time of your use of the product or service, language preferences, and cookie data.

  • Content - We collect content from your files and communications in order to provide you with the services you have requested, including: when you share a file in the Virtual Classroom or in the Learning Management System, use the chat features provided in the virtual classroom, or send and receive messages in the Learning Management System.

  • Video and audio recordings - In order to provide our services, we store video and audio archives from the sessions held in the virtual classroom with the explicit consent of the User.

How and Why Do We Process Personal Data?

The Provider collects personal information in various ways. In most cases, we receive information directly from the Users in order to provide them with customer service, including responses to your inquiries. Personal information is collected during the following circumstances:

  • When a User registers on our website
  • When implementing pre-contractual and contractual arrangements
  • In the case of, or in connection with, performance, modification, completion, or termination of the use of a service (when executing requests from Users, in the course of customer service, etc.)
The collection of personal data is done in order to:
  1. Process necessary information so that we may improve and provide the relevant service:
    • We process User and customer data to ensure that the services provided are consistent with the concluded contracts.
    • We process User and customer data so that we can provide them with the agreed upon services.
    • We process User and customer data for customer service purposes in view of performance of the agreed services, including for the purpose of the identification (including physical one) of each User by our customers for the normal pursuit of their business activities.
  2. Fulfill legal obligations:
    • We process personal data in accordance with applicable law, which states that we are obliged to provide information to competent authorities.
    • We process the data of our Users when we are obliged to assist competent state and/or municipal authorities in conducting inspections.
    • We process consumer data in order to comply with obligations arising from accounting and tax legislation.
  3. Process data that is necessary to protect the interests of the Provider:
    • We process personal data when conducting internal analyses to improve the services offered by the Provider and to develop new ones.
    • We process the personal data of consumers to investigate consumer satisfaction with the Provider's services.
    • We process the personal data of Users in marketing campaigns, subject to the explicit consent of the User to be involved in such initiatives.
    • We process personal data when it is necessary to settle legal disputes.
    • We process personal data if we consider it necessary to investigate, protect, or take action in connection with illegal or suspected illegal activities, as well as to protect and assert the rights, property, or security of the Provider.

The confidentiality and security of your information is of paramount significance to us. We use industry-standard physical, technical, and administrative security measures that ensure the confidentiality and security of your personal information, which we will not share with Third Parties except in the cases stated in this statement or when we are confident that such sharing is necessary in particular cases, such as preventing abuse or malicious action. Data Protection for Minors

We are unconditionally confident in the protection of the personal data of minors. In this regard, we do not collect or maintain information on our Site about minors without the consent of their parents or guardians. If you are underage or a minor, please discuss your intention to use our services with your parents or guardians. We will take the necessary steps to remove personal information about a minor that has been submitted to our Site without parental or a guardian's consent as soon as we learn of the existence of such personal information.
